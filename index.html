Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Six screens. All the data. Updated every hour when the AI engine completes its cycle.

01

Live Portfolio Value

Total portfolio value, available capital, total PnL in dollars and percent — updated every hour when the engine runs.

02

Open Positions

Every live trade with entry price, current price, unrealized PnL, stop loss, take profit, and strategy tag in real time.

03

AI Signal Feed

Every signal the engine generated — acted on or not. Full indicator values (EMA gap, RSI, MACD, Z-score) logged.

04

Trade History

Complete closed trade log. Realized PnL, exit reason (stop/target/timeout), fees, and duration for every trade.

05

Market Intelligence

Live crypto prices, global forex, precious metals, and African currency pairs: USD/KES, USD/ZAR, and more.

06

Performance Chart

30-day portfolio value chart, daily PnL, cumulative returns, drawdown tracking, win rate, and Sharpe ratio.
